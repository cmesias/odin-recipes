The Best Pancake Recipe

Description

Enjoy this delicious old-fashioned pancakes recipe made with simple ingredients.

This recipe will include flour, baking powder, white sugar, an egg, melted butter, milk, and a pinch of salt for flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups of flour
  • 3.5 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/4 pinch of salt

Prep

  1. In a large bowl, add your flour, baking powder, sugar, egg, melted butter, milk and a pinch of salt.
  2. Whisk everything together until your batter has a thick consistency.

Cooking

  1. Add 2 tablespoons of oil (or butter) in a pan on medium-high heat.
  2. Pour about 1/4 cup of your batter onto the pan.
  3. Flip when one side is golden-brown, then repeat on other side.
  4. Repeat until batter is all used, then enjoy with some syrup, and a little butter on top!
