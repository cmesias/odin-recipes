The Best Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

This easy recipe will show you how to make a delicious grilled cheese sandwich! Great for breakfast or brunch.

We will be using bread, American cheese, mayonnaise and some butter to create this delicious recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces of bread
  • 2 slices of American cheese
  • 4 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 4 tablespoons of butter

Prep

  1. Add 2 tablespoons of butter in a pan on medium heat.
  2. Coat all sides of the bread with mayonnaise.

Cooking

  1. Lay 2 peices of bread on pan.
  2. Add a slice of cheese on each bread
  3. Put a piece of bread on the bread that has cheese.
  4. Flip when toasted golden-brown color.
  5. Wait until other side is also toasted golden brown.
  6. Done!
